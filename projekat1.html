For Ibrahimović age is just a number

After serious injury before 2 years everyone said that Ibrahimović's career is over. Everyone except him. After end of his Manchester United journey and move to LA Galaxy. For most of us it was a sign that his career is over, but he said that the end is when he said it is. Now over his American part of career and 56 goals and 17 assists he proved that we all were wrong. Now he is a step ahead of a next european destination and a real test for him, but for sure, the end is when he said it is.
